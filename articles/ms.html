Minesweeper is a puzzle game where you open all cells on the board while avoiding mines. The goal of the game is to open all cells without mines.

You can place a flag on a cell by right-clicking. Flags can be placed where you think there might be a mine. Cells with a flag will be marked with an "F" and will be locked to prevent accidental opening. Flags can be removed by right-clicking again.

Each opened cell will display the number of mines in the 8 adjacent cells (nothing will be displayed if the number is 0). Left-click a cell with a number, and if there are the same number of flagged adjacent cells, the remaining cells will automatically open.

The game can be played at 4 different difficulty levels. Each level - Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, and Superhuman - has different board sizes and numbers of mines.

Difficulty Rows Columns Number of Mines
Beginner 9 9 10
Intermediate 16 16 40
Advanced 16 30 99
Super Advanced 50 50 500
Ultra Advanced 100 100 2000


To start the game, select the difficulty and click the "Start Game" button. The first cell you open is programmed to always be free of mines. To reset the board, click the "Start Game" button again.
